Mamoru Hosoda

Personal Information

Immediately after graduating from college, Hosoda went into animation and completely immersed himself in film. He gained recognition for his work in the first two Digimon movies and the sixth One Piece movie

Style

Hosoda does not have a fixed style, but he is always very consistent in making films that are entertaining and popular with a wide variety of audiences.

Notable Films

Hosoda's The Boy and The Beast (2015) is nominated for the 43rd Annie Awards. It is one of his first films to receive international recognition.

Random Fact

Hosoda studied oil painting at the Kanazawa College of Art.
