Satoshi Kon

Personal Information

As a child, Kon read a lot of manga and watched a lot of anime, thus he aspired to be an animator. In college, Kon gained recognition as a manga artist and eventually found his way into film.

After scripting an episode for an anime called "Jojo's Bizarre Adventure", Kon went on to direct his first film, Perfect Blue, which won great acclaim. Until his death in 2010, he continued to produce works that awed critics.

Style

Kon's films mostly focus on blending fantasy and reality. The films always start with both the character and the audience being able to distinguish between fantasy and reality, but by the end of the movies, the two are indistinguishable.

Notable Films

Kon's Perfect Blue (1997) received great acclaim and, as a result, raised the expectations for his next films.

Random Fact

Kon was always fascinated by the human mind and its complexity, which is what he based Paprika off of.
